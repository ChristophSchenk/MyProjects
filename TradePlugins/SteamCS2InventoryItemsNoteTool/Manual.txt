When the "save" button for your notes does not work, that is most likely related to your browser storage.
You can look up whats taking up the space by Pressing "F12" and entering this into the console:

var _lsTotal = 0,
    _xLen, _x;
for (_x in localStorage) {
    if (!localStorage.hasOwnProperty(_x)) {
        continue;
    }
    _xLen = ((localStorage[_x].length + _x.length) * 2);
    _lsTotal += _xLen;
    console.log(_x.substr(0, 50) + " = " + (_xLen / 1024).toFixed(2) + " KB")
};
console.log("Total = " + (_lsTotal / 1024).toFixed(2) + " KB");


You can clear ALL data Safed to fix this, that also means your NOTES WILL BE DELETED:
Press "F12" and type: "localStorage.clear();"
On Firefox Maybe: window.localStorage.clear();
